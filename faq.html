Questions, Gently Answered

A few things families
often need to know.

Memorial decisions are rarely made in ordinary circumstances. These answers are here to make the process clearer, calmer, and easier to trust.

If You Are Unsure

A simple message is enough.

You do not need to know exactly what to ask. If your question is personal to your situation, we are always happy to guide you directly.

Contact Memory Stones

When should I order an impression kit?

If you may want a print taken directly, it is best to order as early as possible. If a clinic or funeral home has already taken a print, we may be able to work from that instead.

Can impressions be taken after passing?

Often, yes, though the timing varies with the circumstances. If you are unsure, please reach out as soon as you can and we will let you know what may still be possible.

Do you work with vets and funeral homes?

Yes. We support both direct family orders and partner-facilitated memorials, including situations where a print has already been taken with care.

What if I already have a print?

If you already have a usable paw print, fingerprint, or handprint, send us the details and we can confirm whether it appears suitable before you proceed.

How long does production take?

Timeframes depend on whether we are waiting on a returned impression, reviewing engraving details, or beginning the final handmade piece. We aim to communicate clearly at each stage.

Will every stone look exactly the same?

No. Each Memory Stone is individually handcrafted, and slight variation is part of the character of the memorial. The goal is to preserve the distinct impression, not to make something generic.

Can I send one as a gift?

Yes, although many families prefer to arrange the memorial directly. If you are considering a gift, contact us first so we can advise on the most appropriate option.

What happens if there is a problem with my order?

If there has been damage in transit or a genuine workmanship issue, contact us promptly and we will review the most appropriate resolution, including repair or remake where justified.

Is my information handled privately?

Yes. Memorial details, print materials, and personal information should be handled with discretion. We use them only for the purpose of supporting and completing your order unless otherwise agreed.

Do you ship across Australia?

Yes. We currently ship Australia-wide. If you need delivery outside Australia, contact us before ordering so we can confirm what may be possible.
