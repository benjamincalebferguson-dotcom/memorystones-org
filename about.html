About Memory Stones

Created for remembrance
that feels lasting.

Memory Stones began from a simple conviction: when someone has been deeply loved, remembrance should not feel generic. In moments of loss, many families long for something they can still hold. We created Memory Stones to offer a memorial that feels grounded, personal, and enduring.

Why This Exists

A memorial with presence,
not excess.

Many memorial products are designed either as decorative objects or standard keepsakes. Memory Stones was created for those seeking something quieter and more substantial: a piece shaped by touch, and made to sit naturally in everyday life.

That is why each memorial begins with an impression that is recognisably theirs. A paw print. A fingerprint. A handprint. Something intimate, personal, and impossible to mistake for anyone else.

We work in small numbers and with a careful pace. Our aim is not only to make a beautiful object, but to make the process feel steadier for the people moving through it.

What Guides The Work

Calm, private,
and well made.

  • Stone was chosen for its weight, permanence, and quiet presence in the home.
  • Each memorial is individually handcrafted rather than batch produced.
  • Families, funeral homes, and veterinary teams are supported with direct communication.
  • The 51% Impact pledge reflects a permanent commitment, not a temporary campaign.
How We Work

Made with care from first enquiry
through to delivery.

01

Personal guidance

Some families know exactly what they need. Others are unsure what is possible. We help quietly, without pressure, and explain the next step clearly.

02

Print-led memorials

03

Respectful handling

Memorial details, names, dates, and print materials are handled with the discretion this kind of work requires.

If you are considering a memorial and are unsure where to begin, a simple message is enough. We are always happy to guide you quietly and clearly.
