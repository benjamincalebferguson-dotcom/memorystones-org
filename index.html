The Process

Simple to order.
Crafted to last forever.

Whether you're honouring a beloved pet or a cherished person, the process is thoughtful and simple — we guide you every step of the way.

01
Order Your Kit

Choose your memorial type — pet or human — and order your impression kit online. It arrives within days, including everything you need to capture the print.

02
Capture the Print

Use the kit to take a gentle impression of a paw print or fingerprint. The kit works at all stages — before or after passing, in partnership with your vet or funeral home.

03
We Craft Your Stone

Your impression is hand-cast into a smooth, weighted memorial stone. Each stone is individually poured, sanded, and finished — no two are identical.

04
Delivered to You

Your finished Memory Stone arrives in a keepsake box, ready to hold, display, or place where they were most loved. A living tribute to a life fully lived.

About Memory Stones

Created for remembrance
that feels lasting.

Memory Stones began from a simple belief: when someone has been deeply loved, remembrance should not feel generic. In the midst of loss, many people are looking not for more things, but for one meaningful object that still carries a sense of closeness.

That is why each piece is shaped around touch: a paw print, a fingerprint, a handprint. We work quietly, in small numbers, so the memorial can feel considered from first enquiry through to the moment it arrives.

Our role is not only to make a beautiful object, but to make the process feel steadier. Families, funeral homes, and veterinary teams trust us with details that are deeply personal. We try to honour that trust in the way we communicate, craft, package, and deliver every stone.

What guides the work

Premium memorial pieces should feel grounded, not ornamental. Every part of the experience is designed to feel calm, private, and worthy of the life being remembered.

Stone was chosen for its weight, permanence, and quiet presence in the home.
Each memorial is individually handcrafted rather than batch produced.
We support both direct family orders and professional partner referrals.
Our 51% Impact pledge reflects a long-term commitment to doing good beyond the product itself.

Questions, Gently Answered

A few things families
often need to know.

Memorial decisions are rarely made in ordinary circumstances. These answers are here to make the process clearer, calmer, and easier to trust.

If your question is personal to your situation, or you are unsure what may still be possible, please contact us directly. We are always happy to guide you quietly and clearly.

When should I order?

If you would like to capture a print yourself, it is best to order the impression kit as soon as possible. If your vet or funeral home has already taken a print, we may be able to work from that instead.

Can a print be taken after passing?

In many cases, yes. The exact timing depends on the circumstances, so we encourage you to reach out or speak with your veterinary or funeral care team if you are unsure.

Do you work with funeral homes and veterinary clinics?

Yes. We regularly support partners who help families collect impressions with dignity and care, or who provide an existing print for us to work from.

How long does the process take?

Timeframes vary depending on the product and whether we are waiting on a returned kit or supplied print. We aim to communicate clearly at each step so you know what to expect.

Will the finished stone look exactly like the original print?

Each stone is handmade, so slight variation is part of the piece's character. Our goal is not to make something generic, but to preserve the distinct impression that made them recognisably theirs.

What if something is wrong with my order?

If a product arrives damaged or there has been an issue in workmanship, please contact us promptly. We will review the situation with care and discuss repair, remake, or another appropriate resolution.
