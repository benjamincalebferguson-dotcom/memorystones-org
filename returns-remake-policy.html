Policy

Returns & Remake Policy

Memory Stones pieces are custom memorial items created to order. Because of their highly personal nature, returns are handled differently from standard retail goods. Our approach is to be fair, careful, and thoughtful if something has gone wrong.

Last updated: 5 April 2026

Change of Mind

Change-of-mind returns are generally not available for custom-made products, including finished Memory Stones created from a specific paw print, fingerprint, handprint, inscription, or other memorial detail. This is because each piece is made specifically for the person or companion being remembered.

If There Is a Workmanship Issue

If your order arrives damaged, or if you believe there has been a genuine issue in workmanship, please contact us promptly with your order details and supporting photographs where possible. We will review the matter carefully and discuss the most appropriate resolution, which may include repair, partial replacement, or remake.

Natural Variation

Each Memory Stone is individually handcrafted. Slight differences in tone, finish, edge detail, or the way a print presents in the final piece are part of the handmade character of the memorial and do not, on their own, constitute a defect.

Suitability of Source Material

The final outcome of a memorial stone depends in part on the quality and clarity of the source impression or print supplied. If we believe supplied material may affect the result, we will do our best to advise you before production proceeds.

Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this policy excludes, restricts, or modifies any rights you may have under Australian Consumer Law. Where a remedy is required by law, Memory Stones will provide that remedy.
