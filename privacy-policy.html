Policy

Privacy Policy

Memory Stones handles personal information with discretion and care. Many enquiries involve grief, family details, and sensitive memorial information, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Last updated: 5 April 2026

What We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, order details, and any memorial information you choose to provide. This may include the name of the person or companion being remembered, dates, inscription requests, and print-related details.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, process orders, create and deliver memorial products, communicate about production or shipping, and improve our services. We may also use your details for essential record-keeping and administrative purposes.

Prints and Memorial Materials

Where you provide a paw print, fingerprint, handprint, impression, or related reference material, we use that material only for the purpose of assessing, preparing, and completing your order, unless otherwise agreed with you. We handle these materials respectfully and restrict access to them to the extent reasonably practicable.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers where reasonably necessary to operate our business, such as payment processors, website platforms, shipping carriers, or professional advisers. We may also share information where required by law.

Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, but we aim to use reputable tools and sensible administrative safeguards.

Access and Corrections

If you would like to access or correct personal information we hold about you, please contact us using the details on our website. We will respond within a reasonable time, subject to any lawful limitations.

Cookies and Website Data

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality, analytics, and site improvement. You can adjust your browser settings if you prefer to limit or disable cookies, though some website features may be affected.
