Shipping Policy

We know many Memory Stones orders are placed during tender and time-sensitive circumstances. Our aim is to communicate clearly, dispatch carefully, and keep you informed throughout the process.

Last updated: 5 April 2026

Order Stages

Some orders involve more than one stage. For example, an impression kit may be sent first, then returned to us before the final memorial stone is produced. Shipping time therefore depends on the product ordered and whether we are waiting on materials from you or your care provider.

Dispatch of Impression Kits

Where impression kits are in stock, we aim to dispatch them promptly after payment. Dispatch times may vary during public holidays, busy seasonal periods, or where order details need clarification before shipment.

Dispatch of Finished Memory Stones

Finished stones are custom memorial pieces made to order. Production begins once we have received a suitable impression or approved print details. Because each stone is individually handcrafted, dispatch timing can vary. We will provide estimated timeframes and notify you if meaningful delays arise.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship across Australia. If you require shipping outside Australia, please contact us before ordering so we can confirm whether international delivery is available and appropriate for your order.

Delivery Partners and Risk

Orders are shipped using reputable delivery providers selected by Memory Stones. Risk in the goods passes to you upon delivery to the nominated address, to the extent permitted by law. If your parcel arrives damaged, please contact us as soon as possible and include photographs where available.

Incorrect Addresses

Please ensure your delivery details are entered carefully. Memory Stones is not responsible for delays or losses caused by incorrect address information supplied at checkout or by email. If you realise an error has been made, contact us immediately and we will do our best to assist before dispatch.

Questions About Timing

If your order is connected to a funeral, memorial service, veterinary timeline, or another time-sensitive event, please contact us before ordering where possible. We will let you know what is realistically achievable.
