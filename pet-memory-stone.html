Pet Memorial

An enduring memorial for the life
that changed your home.

Each Pet Memory Stone is hand-poured and carefully finished to preserve the distinctive impression of your companion's paw. Weighted, smooth, and quietly substantial, it is designed to be held, displayed, or kept close in a place of remembrance.

Why It Feels Different

Quietly substantial.

  • Created from your pet's own paw impression
  • Individually handcrafted rather than mass produced
  • Designed to feel beautiful in the hand and understated in the home
  • Suitable for private remembrance or memorial display
If You Do Not Yet Have A Print

There is still a first step.

If you do not yet have a paw impression, we can help you begin with an impression kit. If your veterinary clinic has already taken a print, we may be able to work from that instead.

For many families, the comfort lies in having something tangible and lasting. A Memory Stone is designed to feel natural in the home and deeply personal in meaning, without becoming ornate or overstated.
